Letters to Daniel

Ever since I can remember myself I love cinema. Because of that, I went to “Thelma yellin” high-school of the arts, where I majored in Cinema. My final project was a documentary about the process of creating a film. I have chosen this project after my cousin Sacha Grunpeter , who was a successful actor and screenwriter, died in a car accident. I admired him, he was a role model for me and I dreamed that when I’ll be older , I could share my projects with him and even create with him.

On the morning of July 6th 2005 , Los Angeles time , Sasha was on his way to shoot the last scene of “Tracing Cowboys” .. that morning , on his way to the location, his car overturned , he didn't make it from this accident.

After that, I had to investigate the reasons why film-making was so important to me?

Making this film help me deal with the death of my role model and dream. This was the last film I have created, I have dedicated it to Sacha.
