With the IES Inria team we have conceptualized of the software deposit workflow when it comes to archiving software artefacts.
This curation workflow was developed to help digital archivist handle a new and peculiar artefact - software source code. To that end, a set of specific guidelines have been developed and incorporated into technical guides for the moderation process.
I presented the moderation process to the HAL moderators on September 20th, 2018.
